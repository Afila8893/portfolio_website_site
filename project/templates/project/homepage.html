About the National Service Scheme

The National Service Scheme (NSS) is a student-centered program that complements education by linking academic learning with community service. Launched on September 24, 1969, in honor of Mahatma Gandhi's birth centenary, NSS fosters the spirit of voluntary work among students and teachers through sustained community interaction.
